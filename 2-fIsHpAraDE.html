Description for the Jury

Let’s bring open-ended recycled materials out, set up a workspace, and invite participants to make sea creatures. We’ll provide repurposed translucent materials and led lights, and creature workshop hours. Participants then invited to gather Saturday at dusk to swim our way through Pyroplolis: fIsH pAraDE (This could also swim past perimeter on burn night if there was interest in this before the start of conclave?)

Description for the Website

The community is invited to make sea creatures with us on Saturday afternoon (byo inspiration, we supply all the repurposed materials and lights) and then swim a glowing, blinking undersea world through Pyropolis after dusk. Appropriately themed attire/acoutrements/musicians wildly encouraged. fIsH pAraDE!
