30 - Moth

Description for the Jury

Loops of Recorded Sanity/Weirdo Overdrive

Description for the Website

This piece will look like a small living room set up in the woods at Flipside. There will be an old school CRT TV connected to a VCR with some items scattered around for it to look inviting. And it will! The purpose is to have a stack of VHS tapes (because how long ago was it that we dealt with magnetic tape medium? Never ever, for some younger burners!) and on those VHS tapes I'm going to have a collection of oddball things on them. I've been experimenting with transferring modern media onto VHS tapes. I want to do that for a number of different things (cartoons, anime, internet videos, cats being cats, etc) and have them transferred onto VHS and then whoever is playing them in the woods can take a moment to appreciate them. We've gotten so comfortable with all this available media at our fingertips but I want to make a new-fangled digital post-VHS age retro reminder of our bygone tape-storage roots.
