9 - Friendly Universal Telecom payphones are back!

Description for the Jury

Pretty sure you all know who FUTelecom is, so I'll just say we've made some big advances in our tech and volunteer edumacations, and we're ready to bring back all 6 payphones and start working on the next! We found a way to do the batteries that fits in the booth and will last us many burns instead of failing after 3 or 4 years like lead acid batteries tend to. $200 per booth for the batteries, and a bit more for chargers and the like to use them. Anything the community gives us will help! We look forward to bringing the FU to the burn as well as the volunteer safety infrastructure!
