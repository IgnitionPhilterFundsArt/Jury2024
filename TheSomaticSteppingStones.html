The Somatic Stepping Stones

Description for the Jury

The design of the Somatic Stepping Stones was deeply influenced by the intricate beauty of these colorful marine creatures. Emmulating the flowing lines and organic shapes, the materials have been carefully selected to mimic the soft and flowing texture of the sea creature's tentacles. Using long, luxurious fibers, each piece will have a deep and plush pile, reminiscent of the gentle undulations of anemone tentacles in the ocean currents. Throughout the design process, I have been continuously referring back to images and of sea anemones and sea slugs, allowing their graceful forms to inform and inspire my creative decisions.
