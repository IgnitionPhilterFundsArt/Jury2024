Wally's Wagon

Description for the Jury

Wally's Wagon will be an inviting beacon, beckoning hungry Flipizens to Wally's Kitchen, where they'll discover not just a meal, but nourishment for both body and soul. Wally generously feeds the masses at Flipside and other events. We want to honor his generosity with art that will sit next to the entrance of kitchen. The chuck wagon is a long standing symbol of cowboy life, and Wally's Kitchen is an extension of that symbol. Our wagon will be made of wood with an illuminated wagon cover, combing the quintessential architypes of Cast Iron Village - Cowboys & Hippies! [Note from Ignition Philter-the application is not from Wally]
