Isla Verde

Description for the Jury

Have you ever gone to the river and thought " behold, a verdant island I see? No? Well that"s all about to change. Plant Camp would like to bring you a floating tropical oasis to heat up your imaginations as you cool down. We will build a green island for the river to lush up your days and light up your nights while, at the very least, holding your beer. We plan to build a base of wooden scaffolding and then install an array of real and fake plants, including a centerpiece coconut tree, to bring a little beauty and natural cheer to the river experience. 80 inches by 96 inches at the base and covered with plants to 48+ inches high.
