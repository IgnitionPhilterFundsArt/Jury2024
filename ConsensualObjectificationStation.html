Consensual Objectification Station

Description for the Jury

A mobile interactive experience that allows people to compliment friends and strangers in a familiar but socially discouraged way- by objectifying them! Objectification is normally viewed as dehumanizing since it focuses on one small part of a person at a time, but this art piece seeks to reclaim this experience and allow the objectifier and objectified to connect by allowing the objectification to be viewed as a compliment. This art piece will consist of a pedestal for the consenting human to stand upon to be objectified, a lemonade stand-like structure for the main objectifier to present themselves in (especially handy for when the piece is set up in one location so passers-by can participate), a lit marquee-style arrow to point at the objectified human (which is attached to a wagon so we can easily move the art piece around the city), and a spotlight and megaphone to heighten the objectification experience for those near and far.
