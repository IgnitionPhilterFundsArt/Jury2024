7 - Ring In The Year Of The Dragon

Description for the Jury

OVERVIEW: I am writing to submit a proposal for an art grant to fund the creation of a high-striker (“ring the bell”) carnival game that will also be a functional flame effect. This project aims to combine artistic expression with interactive elements to engage and inspire Flipizens. The installation combines all elements of the theme for the 2024 event, Cosmic Critter Carnival. The aesthetic elements of the piece interpret Cosmic Critter through the Chinese zodiac. In the Chinese lunar new year, 2024 has transitioned into the year of the dragon. The installation will challenge burners to test their strength in traditional carnival fashion. ARTISTIC VISION: The installation seeks to encapsulate the celebratory aspects of both Eastern lunar new year festivals and Western carnival atmosphere. The 2-dimensional representation of the pagoda and dragon will be detailed to mimic the beautiful intricacy of Chinese architecture and art, while the lighting and interactive elements will draw participants in with fun-loving carnival energy. Participants will use a mallet to slam down on a lever, launching a striker mechanism, attempting to reach and ring the gong that adorns the top of the tower. Each roof section of the pagoda will be marked by other zodiac animals and will illuminate if the striker passes them. Upon reaching the top, the striker will not only ring the gong, but evoke a fireball to burst upward from the mouth of the dragon.
