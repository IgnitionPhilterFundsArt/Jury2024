31 - Noh Me, Know Mu

Description for the Jury

"Step into the ethereal realm of Noh Me, Know Mu at Burning Flipside, where the essence of creation thrives in the absence of creators. An esoterically artistic space, whose existential emptiness lures the effusive or indifferent artists and artisans to silence its vacuous echo with their ethereal tapestry of emancipated artistic expression and communal spirit. Noh theater will consist of a 12x20 structure decorated in a way befitting of the nothingness, featuring 2 skeletal horses (from my rocking horse sculptures). The space will have lights and sound (amplified, but we're not a dance camp, tho I can't promise there won't be dancing), and a bar. Our camp will host a couple of happy hour events* and a dance workshop in the space, but outside of that Noh will serve as an open invitation community event space. We will promote the space in the months before Flipside, create a calendar of events, and publish it. * Some possible camp hosted events: 1. How to Suck Less Happy Hour, where we might serve adult Capri Suns 2. Old Timers Lawn Hang, where we will invite all the old school Flipizens to sit on the lawn and yell at young'uns to """"get off our lawn"""" while we drink iced coffee and discuss how it's just not as good as it used be. . . . . more to come . . . ."
