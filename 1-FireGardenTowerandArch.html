1 - Fire Garden Tower and Arch

Description for the Jury

My grant request is for propane and trailer rental to bring out my 18' metal fire tower flame effect and a 15' arch to serve as the 'gate' into the Fire Garden where burners can check out the effects. The Fire Garden Tower is a structure that has a 1.25" valved flame effect fed by 2 100lb high pressure propane sources into 2 30 gal. accumulators. The vent at the top shoots the flame into a horizontal 5' diameter flat plate so that the flame blossoms out into a disc at 18' in the air. There's an additional mystery component I'm experimenting with that will add extra colored flames (if it works.) The Fire Tower has a remote 'big red button' on a stand so that folks can fire it off from a safe distance.

Safety Plan

The Fire Tower has a remote switch stand so that viewers do not need to approach within 15' of the effect. The effect controller (at the base of the tower,) has a key lock, an emergency cutoff and is contained in a closable and lockable case. The effect has 1/4 shutoff valves at multiple stages allowing quick cutoff of vapor. A 10' exclusion ring around the effect is designated with safety tape. The effect controller is removed from the effect when not in use. The effect has been constructed to NFPA-160 standards and would pass a State or local Fire Marshal inspection if this were a permitted event.
