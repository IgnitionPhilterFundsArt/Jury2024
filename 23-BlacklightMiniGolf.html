23 - Blacklight Mini Golf

Description for the Jury

Hello jury, thank you so much for your consideration. For my birthday last year I built an entire 9 hole blacklight mini golf course in my backyard by myself. It was an insane amount of work but really beautiful and so incredibly rewarding. Many, many friends played through. Perhaps one of you played through as well? The entire evening I just fantasized about bringing the project to Flipside, only better, and that is what I hope to do. It will be functional every evening from dusk until dawn, assuming it is not raining in which case I would have to put the blacklights away. I love fun. Don't you love fun? Let's play!

Description for the Website

Did you forget to leave your competitive side at home? Well, bring your friends and show them who the real winner is. Blacklight mini golf is available every evening from dusk until dawn for your entertainment. Can you beat par?
