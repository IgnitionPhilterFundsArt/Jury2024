37 - 3am Eats

Description for the Jury

"3am Eats purpose is creating and serving Eats to on-duty volunteers and the general population that is awake at 3am on the Friday, Saturday, Sunday and Monday mornings. Meals* to include: Chili w/ Firehouse Mash Taters, Grilled Cheese Sandwiches, Cheeseburgers, Chicken Noodle Soup with rolls. * Meals may change depending on funding, ease of meal, assistance, and availability of available food stock at the stores - it will all be tried and tested good. I work the graveyard shift at UT and camp with Wally (Cast Iron Village at FZBN, and Cowboys & Hippies at FlipSide), and will be staying up all night until 8am (my usual bedtime) to keep my body on my 'normal' sleep / awake cycle. At FRBN 2024, I did 3am Chili with Firehouse Mash Taters (I’m an old retired firefighter), and we served about 45-60 people and it was a huge success, and I’d like to expand this to creating and serving each night (early morning) at FlipSide."

Description for the Website

"3am Eats - Serving Eats at 3am Burner Time at Cowboys & Hippies (also know as Wally’s Cast Iron Kitchen) for volunteers, night owls, cowboys, hippies and everyone who may be up at 3am and needs some sustenance. * On Duty Volunteers GET Front of the LINE Service - this is my way of thanking them for making FlipSide happen. * * Bring your own bowl, cup, spoon etc…. aka: radical self-reliance."
