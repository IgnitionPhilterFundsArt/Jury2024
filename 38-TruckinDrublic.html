38 - Truck in Drublic

Description for the Jury

This is an art car request. We recently had to replace parts of our burn truck with unmatchung colored parts.. we covered it in stickers collected at burns and the punk truck was born. If we could get funding for a dance pole and some lights we could turn this into a dancing art car.

No fire but this is mobile art and it is climbable. We are going to cover the rails in something soft to avoid head injury. We also have a welder in our camp to help make sure the pole is secure.
